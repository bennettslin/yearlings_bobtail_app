# Contractions
* it'll
* it'd (for "it had" or "it would")
* that'll
* that'd (for "that had" or "that would")
* what'll
* what'd (for "what had" or "what did", not for "what would")
* I'd (also he, she, they, who)
* might've (also would, could, should)
* means (without that)

# Rules
* If ambiguity between "had" or "would," or "had" and "did"
    * Verb to clarify which one must immediately follow
    * Or, if verb is "be" or "been," can have adverb in between
